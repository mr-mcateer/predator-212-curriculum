MOST CRITICAL CHECKPOINT
================================================================================

‚≠ê WEEK 3 WEDNESDAY - VALVE LASH ADJUSTMENT

Video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ixVMH0u7C34 (watch TWICE)

SPECS:
  Intake:  0.003" - 0.005"
  Exhaust: 0.004" - 0.006"

PROCEDURE:
1. Position at Top Dead Center (TDC)
2. Use feeler gauge
3. Adjust rocker screw until snug
4. Record measurement
5. Rotate 180¬∞ and check other valve
6. Record both measurements

ACTION: DO NOT PROCEED to reassembly without:
  ‚Ä¢ Instructor verification
  ‚Ä¢ Both valve lash measurements correct
  ‚Ä¢ Student understanding (not just lucky)
  ‚Ä¢ Worksheet signed off

‚ö†Ô∏è This is why 80% of engines won't start if wrong. Get it right.

TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK FIXES
================================================================================

Engine won't start?
‚Üí Check: Spark ‚Üí Fuel ‚Üí VALVE LASH (most common)
‚Üí See: docs/FAQ.md

Stuck bolt?
‚Üí Apply penetrating oil, wait 15-30 min
‚Üí Steady pressure, don't jerk

Stripped threads?
‚Üí Can't fix easily - document in notebook
‚Üí Discuss with student

Student confused?
‚Üí See: docs/FAQ.md
‚Üí Show relevant video section
