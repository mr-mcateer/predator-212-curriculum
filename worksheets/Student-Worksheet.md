## PROJECT OVERVIEW

This project involves complete disassembly and reassembly of a Predator 212cc four-stroke engine. This is one of the most common small engines used in everything from mini-bikes to portable generators.

**Estimated Time:** 20-30 hours of hands-on work
**Skill Level:** Intermediate
**Safety Rating:** Requires strict adherence to safety procedures

