## Grading Criteria

Your final grade is based on these five components:

---

## 1. SAFETY ADHERENCE & PROCEDURES FOLLOWED (30%)

**Excellent (A: 27-30 points)**
- All safety procedures followed exactly as written
- No shortcuts or risky behavior observed
- Proper PPE used throughout project
- All instructor checkoffs obtained at required points
- No safety violations or concerns

**Good (B: 24-26 points)**
- Safety procedures generally followed
- 1-2 minor safety reminders needed
- Proper PPE mostly used
- All critical checkoffs obtained
- No major safety violations

**Satisfactory (C: 21-23 points)**
- Most safety procedures followed
- 3-4 safety reminders needed
- PPE used inconsistently
- Some critical checkoffs obtained
- Minor safety violations but nothing dangerous

**Needs Improvement (D: 18-20 points)**
- Several safety procedures not followed
- Multiple safety reminders needed
- PPE not consistently used
- Critical checkoffs missed
- Safety concerns present

**Unsatisfactory (F: <18 points)**
- Serious safety violations
- Refused to follow procedures
- No PPE used
- Critical checkoffs refused
- Unsafe behavior that could cause damage or injury

---

## 2. DOCUMENTATION COMPLETENESS (25%)

**Excellent (A: 22-25 points)**
- Notebook has ALL required sections filled out completely
- Every component documented with name, function, condition
- All measurements recorded (ring gaps, bore, bearing condition)
- Valve lash attempts all recorded (including failed attempts)
- All torque specs recorded as actually used
- Photos/sketches included where applicable
- Handwriting/formatting is neat and organized

**Good (B: 19-21 points)**
- Notebook has most sections completed
- Most components documented
- Most measurements recorded
- Valve lash attempts recorded
- Most torque specs recorded
- Minor organization issues but mostly legible

**Satisfactory (C: 16-18 points)**
- Notebook has key sections completed
- Basic components documented
- Key measurements recorded (may be missing some)
- Valve lash attempts recorded but incomplete
- Some torque specs missing
- Some legibility issues

**Needs Improvement (D: 13-15 points)**
- Notebook has only basic sections
- Incomplete component documentation
- Several measurements missing
- Valve lash attempts incomplete or unclear
- Many torque specs missing
- Difficult to read

**Unsatisfactory (F: <13 points)**
- Notebook incomplete or missing
- Minimal documentation
- Most measurements missing
- Valve lash not documented
- Torque specs not recorded
- Cannot read what was attempted

---

## 3. MEASUREMENTS & SPECIFICATIONS ACCURACY (20%)

**Excellent (A: 18-20 points)**
- All measurements recorded accurately
- Actual measurements differ from spec by <5%
- Valve lash within specification (Intake 0.003-0.005", Exhaust 0.004-0.006")
- Torque specs match manual values exactly
- Clear understanding of why specs matter shown in documentation

**Good (B: 16-17 points)**
- Most measurements recorded accurately
- Measurements within acceptable range
- Valve lash within specification
- Torque specs mostly correct
- General understanding of specs shown

**Satisfactory (C: 14-15 points)**
- Key measurements recorded
- Some measurement errors (5-10% variance)
- Valve lash close to specification
- Some torque spec errors
- Basic understanding of specs

**Needs Improvement (D: 12-13 points)**
- Measurements recorded but with errors
- Significant variance (>10% from spec)
- Valve lash outside specification
- Multiple torque spec errors
- Limited understanding of specifications

**Unsatisfactory (F: <12 points)**
- Measurements missing or incorrect
- Large variance from specifications
- Valve lash wrong
- Torque specs not used correctly
- No understanding of why specs matter

---

## 4. ENGINE STARTUP SUCCESS (15%)

**Excellent (A: 13-15 points)**
- Engine starts on first or second attempt
- Runs smoothly with proper sound
- No obvious issues on startup
- Required adjustments minimal
- Documents what happened accurately

**Good (B: 11-12 points)**
- Engine starts after 3-5 attempts
- Runs but may need minor adjustment
- One or two issues resolved during startup
- Documents startup process clearly

**Satisfactory (C: 10 points)**
- Engine starts after multiple attempts (5-10)
- Runs but rough
- Several adjustments needed
- Documents troubleshooting attempts

**Needs Improvement (D: 7-9 points)**
- Engine won't start or starts but dies immediately
- Won't run smoothly
- Multiple failed adjustments
- Poor documentation of attempts

**Unsatisfactory (F: <7 points)**
- Engine does not start
- Refuses to run
- No attempt to troubleshoot
- No documentation of efforts

---

## 5. REFLECTION & LEARNING DEMONSTRATED (10%)

**Excellent (A: 9-10 points)**
- Reflection answers show deep understanding of concepts
- Clearly explains what was learned about engines
- Connects concepts to real-world applications
- Discusses what was hardest and how it was solved
- Thoughtful about what would do differently next time

**Good (B: 8 points)**
- Reflection shows good understanding
- Explains what was learned
- Makes some real-world connections
- Discusses challenges and solutions

**Satisfactory (C: 7 points)**
- Reflection shows basic understanding
- Explains what was learned in simple terms
- Mentions challenges
- Answers most reflection questions

**Needs Improvement (D: 6 points)**
- Reflection is brief or surface-level
- Limited explanation of learning
- Minimal discussion of challenges
- Some reflection questions not answered

**Unsatisfactory (F: <6 points)**
- Reflection incomplete or missing
- Shows minimal understanding
- Does not answer reflection questions
- No evidence of learning
